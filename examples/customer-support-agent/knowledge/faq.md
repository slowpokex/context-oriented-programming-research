# Frequently Asked Questions

## Orders & Shipping

### How long does shipping take?
- **Standard Shipping**: 5-7 business days
- **Express Shipping**: 2-3 business days  
- **Overnight Shipping**: Next business day (order by 2 PM EST)

### How can I track my order?
Once your order ships, you'll receive a tracking email with a link. You can also:
1. Log into your account and view order history
2. Contact support with your order number

### Can I change my shipping address after ordering?
We can update your shipping address if the order hasn't shipped yet. Once shipped, we cannot redirect packages. Contact us immediately if you need to make changes.

### What carriers do you use?
We ship via UPS, FedEx, and USPS depending on your location and selected shipping speed.

### Do you ship internationally?
Yes! We ship to over 50 countries. International shipping typically takes 7-14 business days. Customers are responsible for any customs duties or taxes.

## Returns & Refunds

### What is your return policy?
We accept returns within 30 days of delivery for most items. Items must be:
- Unused and in original packaging
- Accompanied by receipt or order confirmation
- Not a final sale item

### How do I start a return?
1. Log into your account
2. Go to Order History
3. Select the order and click "Return Item"
4. Print the prepaid shipping label
5. Drop off at any UPS location

### How long do refunds take?
- **Credit Card**: 5-10 business days after we receive the return
- **PayPal**: 3-5 business days
- **Store Credit**: Immediate upon return processing

### Do you offer exchanges?
Yes! You can exchange for a different size or color. Contact support to arrange an exchange.

## Products

### How do I know if an item is in stock?
Product pages show current availability. If an item is out of stock, you can sign up for restock notifications.

### Can I get a discount for bulk orders?
Yes, we offer volume discounts for orders of 10+ units. Contact our sales team at sales@company.com.

### Do your products come with a warranty?
Yes, all products include a 1-year limited warranty covering manufacturing defects. See our warranty page for details.

## Account & Payments

### How do I reset my password?
1. Click "Forgot Password" on the login page
2. Enter your email address
3. Check your email for reset instructions
4. Create a new password

### What payment methods do you accept?
- Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover
- PayPal
- Apple Pay, Google Pay
- Shop Pay
- Affirm (buy now, pay later)

### Is my payment information secure?
Absolutely. We use industry-standard SSL encryption and never store your full credit card number. We are PCI DSS compliant.

### How do I update my billing information?
Log into your account, go to Settings > Payment Methods, and update your information.

## Contact & Support

### What are your support hours?
- **Phone**: Mon-Fri, 9 AM - 6 PM EST
- **Chat**: Mon-Fri, 8 AM - 8 PM EST
- **Email**: 24/7 (response within 24 hours)

### How do I escalate an issue?
If you're not satisfied with the initial support resolution, you can:
1. Ask to speak with a supervisor
2. Email escalations@company.com
3. Use our feedback form with reference number

### Do you have a physical store?
We are an online-only retailer, but we have partnerships with select retail locations. Check our Store Locator page.
