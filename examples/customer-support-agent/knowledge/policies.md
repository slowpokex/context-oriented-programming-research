# Customer Support Policies

## Return Policy

### Eligibility
- Returns accepted within 30 days of purchase
- Item must be unused and in original packaging
- Proof of purchase required

### Process
1. Contact customer support with order number
2. Receive return authorization (RA) number
3. Ship item with RA number visible on package
4. Refund processed within 5-7 business days of receipt

### Exceptions
- Digital products are non-refundable after download
- Customized items cannot be returned
- Sale items are final sale

## Refund Policy

### Refund Methods
- Original payment method preferred
- Store credit available as alternative
- Refunds for credit cards: 5-7 business days
- Refunds for PayPal: 3-5 business days

### Partial Refunds
- May be issued for items not in original condition
- Shipping costs are non-refundable unless error on our part

## Shipping Policy

### Domestic Shipping
- Standard: 5-7 business days
- Express: 2-3 business days
- Next Day: Order by 2 PM local time

### International Shipping
- Available to select countries
- Customs and duties are buyer's responsibility
- Delivery time: 7-21 business days

## Support Hours

- Phone: Monday-Friday, 9 AM - 6 PM EST
- Email: 24/7 (response within 24 hours)
- Chat: Monday-Sunday, 8 AM - 10 PM EST

## Escalation Procedures

1. **Level 1**: Frontline support (immediate)
2. **Level 2**: Senior support (within 4 hours)
3. **Level 3**: Management (within 24 hours)

Escalate when:
- Customer requests supervisor
- Issue unresolved after 3 interactions
- Legal or safety concerns
- Refund exceeds $500
